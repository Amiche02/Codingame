Input is a rectangular playground.
You are the * asterisk.
The letters are the other kids in a game of tag.
Who is closest to you?

Note: Distance is measured "as the crow flies" (aka Euclidean distance)
Entrée
Line 1: An integer heightOfPlayground
Next heightOfPlayground lines: Strings (all the same width), representing the playground, including yourself and the other kids
Sortie
Line 1: The kid that is closest to you
Contraintes
• There are no ties. In other words, exactly one kid is closest to you.
• Each kid is represented by a letter: can be uppercase or lowercase. It is case-sensitive.
